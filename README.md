#   MAVENMART: Retail Sales and Profit Analytics with Microsoft Fabric and Power BI

## Overview
A multinational grocery retailer operating across North America manages millions of transactional records generated from daily sales, inventory movements, product returns, purchasing operations, and customer activity across multiple product categories and brands.

As the business expanded, leadership teams faced growing challenges in consolidating and analyzing operational data quickly enough to support strategic decision-making. Manual reporting processes limited visibility into key business metrics such as sales performance, profit margins, product demand, customer purchasing behavior, and return trends across stores and markets.

To improve business intelligence capabilities, an automated analytics solution was developed using Microsoft Fabric and Power BI. Transactional data from multiple operational systems was centralized, transformed, and modeled through a scalable data pipeline, enabling near real-time reporting and performance monitoring.

Interactive dashboards were designed to help executives and sales managers track revenue, profit, transactions, inventory trends, and top-performing product categories, supporting faster and more data-driven business decisions across the retail network.

## Analysis Framework & Visualization
An end-to-end analytics architecture was designed to align operational data with the company’s sales and performance reporting needs.

The solution included:
1.	Defining the reporting workflow and key business metrics in collaboration with sales managers and executive leadership to support strategic decision-making. 
2.	Extracting high-volume transactional data from enterprise storage systems using Python notebooks to automate and scale data ingestion into Microsoft Fabric. 
3.	Building a modern ETL pipeline based on the Medallion Architecture (Bronze, Silver, Gold) using PySpark to clean, transform, and model raw transactional data into analytics-ready datasets. 
4.	Developing interactive Power BI dashboards to monitor 12 key business metrics related to sales, revenue, profit, product performance, and customer trends through time-based comparative analysis.

## Conclusion and Insights
The interactive Power BI dashboard provides executives and retail managers with near real-time visibility into sales performance, revenue growth, profitability, return trends, and regional operations across North America.
The solution enables business teams to:
1.	Monitor business performance from daily to yearly periods to identify seasonal sales patterns, growth opportunities, and low-performing periods.
2.	Analyze top-performing product brands based on transactions, profit margins, and return rates to optimize marketing, pricing, and sales strategies.
3.	Compare operational performance across countries, states, and cities to support regional sales management and market expansion decisions.
4.	Track key financial KPIs such as total revenue, total profit, return volume, and monthly sales trends against business targets in near real time.
By centralizing transactional analytics into a single reporting platform, the company significantly improved reporting efficiency, accelerated decision-making, and strengthened its ability to adjust sales and customer engagement strategies based on data-driven insights.
